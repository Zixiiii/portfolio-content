Enjoy

Scenery

There are many beautiful parks in Shenzhen. You can come to the Sunrise Theater in the morning to watch the sunrise. You can also lie on the lawn near the sea to watch the sunset in the evening.

At the same time, Shenzhen also has a lot of beaches. Blue sky, white clouds, and sunny beaches meet all people's imagination of a good holiday.

Shopping

As a very developed commercial city, Shenzhen has various commercial organizations, such as The Mixc and Wanxiang Tiandi. Shopping in Shenzhen isn't just about buying things, it's a great way to get to know the city from another perspective.
